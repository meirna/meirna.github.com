The (very) short documentary addresses some of the criticism Record Store Day has received in the past few years.

Interviewees include the owner of the well known Glaswegian independent record shop/non-profit organisation New Hellfire Club, and a staff member (who is also a Glasgow-based producer/DJ) of Rubadub, a store that has famously declared their own Rubadub Shop Day in place of Record Store Day.

My roles: camera, script, voiceover, post-production.
